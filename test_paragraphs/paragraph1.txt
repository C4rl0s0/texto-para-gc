La La Land is a 2016 American romantic musical comedy-drama film written and directed by 
Damien Chazelle. Starring Ryan Gosling and Emma Stone, the film focuses on two young people 
struggling to make ends meet in Los Angeles, while pursuing their dreams as artists.
Justin Hurwitz composed the film's musical score, while Linus Sandgren was the cinematographer.
David and Sandy Reynolds-Wasco were responsible for the production design and Mary Zophres designed the costumes.
